Biological Imaging Gallery

This gallery is a portfolio of images that were produced during my time at the Biological Imaging Aprenticeship at Friday Harbor Labs under the supervision of Dr. Adam Summers. During this apprenticeship I learned the following techniques: Histology, Scanning Electron Microscopy, Digital Photography with natural light and UV light, CT scanning and editing, and 3D printing.
