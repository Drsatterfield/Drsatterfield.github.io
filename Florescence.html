Flourescence


An image of a cleared and stained bonnethead embryo, taken under UV light.



I made this design out of cleared and stained clingfish. These are not copy pasted, each fish in actually there. It was really satifying to make because this was the exact number of fish in the jar. This image is also taken under UV light, which causes the alizarin red stain in the fish to flouresce.



This is a naturally flourescent anemone, the image was taken under UV light. This image is unedited.
